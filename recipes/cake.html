cake

Description

This is a delicious cake made with the finest ingredients.

Enjoy a slice of this delightful cake at your next celebration!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp of baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  3. Add the butter, eggs, milk, and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let it cool before serving.
