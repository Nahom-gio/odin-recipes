Description

This is a delicious pasta made with the finest ingredients.

Enjoy a plate of this delightful pasta at your next celebration!

Ingredients

  • 200g of pasta
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 tbsp of olive oil
  • 1/2 cup of grated cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the water in a large pot.
  2. Add the pasta and salt. Cook according to package instructions.
  3. In a separate pan, heat the olive oil and saut√© any desired vegetables.
  4. Drain the pasta and add it to the pan with vegetables.
  5. Toss everything together and sprinkle with grated cheese.
