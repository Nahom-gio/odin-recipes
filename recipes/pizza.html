pizza

Description

This is a delicious pizza made with the finest ingredients.

Enjoy a slice of this delightful pizza at your next celebration!

Ingredients

  • 200g of pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup of pizza sauce
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (428°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese on top.
  6. Add the pepperoni slices and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
